Let us know you’re interested in getting involved in the Amman Valley Community Mosaic Mural !

This project will bring the community together to create a fantastic mural from mosaic. We have already created a brilliant design through Open Community Design Workshops. Throughout this autumn and winter 2024-25, we will be making the mosaic mural in fun, friendly, and FREE Community Mosaic Workshops! Dates to be announced.

To register your interest please:

Register here

The mural will be going onto a brick wall in Glanaman. It’s on your right as you go into the car park by Cwmaman Community Centre and it’s almost opposite Aman Pharmacy.

  • Workshops open to anyone living or working in or near the Amman Valley and age 13+
  • No experience or artistic skills necessary as there will be full expert tuition on hand
  • All materials, tools and refreshments provided
  • Workshops will be held near Garnant

Looking forward to getting creative together!
