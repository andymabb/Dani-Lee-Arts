Amman Valley Community Mosaic Mural

Would you like to be part of a team creating a mural for your community?


This project is bringing the community together to create a fantastic mural from mosaic. We have already created a brilliant design through Community Design Workshops and next we will be making the mural in fun, friendly, and FREE Community Mosaic Workshops! These will start in November and run throughout winter and spring 2024-25. You’re welcome to join at any time.

Book here

  • Workshops open to anyone living or working in/near the Amman Valley and age 13+
  • No experience or artistic skills necessary as there will be full expert tuition on hand
  • All materials, tools and refreshments provided
  • Workshops will be held at the hall next to Bethesda Baptist Chapel, Glanaman

The mural will be going onto a prominent wall in Glanaman. It’s on your right as you go into the car park by Cwmaman Community Centre and it’s almost opposite Aman Pharmacy.

When booking look out for the special day of tile making! This will be led my ceramic artist Jacky John of Carmel pottery and will be a lovely day of making ceramic tiles of our own design to add to the mosaic.
